Simple Process

How PackVote works

From group chaos to perfect itinerary in 3 steps.

01

Create your group

Add your travel crew, set a group name, and share the Group ID. Everyone joins and fills the survey.

02

Everyone votes

Swipe through destination cards. Rank your picks, set your budget, choose your travel style. Takes under 2 minutes.

03

AI builds the plan

Our AI crunches all votes using the Borda Count algorithm and builds a full day-by-day itinerary with weather and costs.
